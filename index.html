About Me

Hello there, my name is Fernando (although I go by a variety of nicknames on the Internet, mainly Fsmeow and Fab). I am currently living in El Salvador and majoring in video-game development and design. I am very interested in programming, graphic design and all computer related things. When I'm not studying I try to keep myself busy on my free time working for others as a freelancer (both as a developer and a designer) or on personal projects that I might find interesting, to get some valuable experience.

Whatever it is I am doing, I am always looking for something new to learn.
